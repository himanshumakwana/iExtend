# iExtend Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-08_

## Summary

iExtend collects no data. None.

## What we don't collect

- No personal information (name, email, phone, address)
- No device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, serial number)
- No usage analytics or behavioral tracking
- No telemetry or diagnostic data by default
- No advertising IDs or ad-network integrations
- No location data
- No contacts, photos, calendar, health, or financial data
- No browsing history

## Network use

iExtend communicates **only** between your iPad and your own computer on
your local Wi-Fi or USB-C network.  No data ever leaves your local network
during normal use.

There are no iExtend servers, no cloud account, no sign-in, no sync.  The
only outbound network traffic from the iPad app is to the IP address of your
computer — which you paired manually.

## Optional crash reports

If — and only if — you opt **in** to crash reporting in Settings → Advanced →
Crash Reports, the app may upload a crash backtrace to a self-hosted server
we operate.  That report contains:

- The crash backtrace (no user data, only memory addresses and symbol names)
- Your device model (e.g. "iPad Pro 11-inch M4")
- Your iPadOS version
- The iExtend app version and build number

We **never** include personal information in crash reports, even when crash
reporting is enabled.  You can disable it at any time in Settings.  Crash
reporting is **off by default**.

## Third-party SDKs and services

None.  iExtend ships no third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs,
social media SDKs, or cloud service client libraries.

## Children's privacy

iExtend does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children
under the age of 13.  Because we collect nothing, there is nothing to
distinguish by age.
